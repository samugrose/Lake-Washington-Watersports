About Us

Lake Washington Watersports provides lessons and hosts wakesurfing sessions in south Lake Washington.

Wake Surf Lessons:

We provide wake surf fun. If you are a beginner and need a lesson to get started, our seasoned pros will get you up and surfing in no time

Wake Surf Sessions:

For the seasoned wake surfer who just wants time to work on that new trick, we provide non-stop surfing action

Equipment Provided:

We provide all the gear, life jackets, surfboards to fit any size and skim boards if you want to try something new. You are welcome to bring your own gear if you have a favorite piece of hardware

Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

    Can beginners participate?
    Yes! Our instructors specialize in teaching first-time surfers. No prior wakeboarding experience is required.

    Is this a group lesson?
    No, all lessons are private. Only your group and the instructor will be on board.

    Where do we meet?
    Gene Coulon Memorial Beach Park public dock, near Ivar’s Acres of Clams Restaurant.

    Booking & Scheduling

    What if I’m late?
    Lessons start promptly. If you arrive late, we cannot extend the time unless availability allows. Please arrive 15 minutes early.

    How much time should I book?
    A minimum of 2 hours is recommended for 3–4 participants.

    What time of day is best?
    Early mornings offer the smoothest conditions; afternoons may be choppier.

    Safety & Requirements

    Do I need to know how to swim?
    Yes, swimming is required to participate. Non-swimmers can join as spectators but cannot surf.

    What should I wear?
    Swimsuits are mandatory. Avoid denim, as it may stain the boat. Bring sunglasses, hats, and layers for cooler conditions.

    On-Board Rules & Policies

    Can I bring food or drinks?
    Drinks are encouraged! Food is permitted only for bookings over 4 hours.

    Can I bring alcohol?
    Responsible drinking is allowed if you’re 21+, but excessive drinking and watersports don’t mix.

    Do you have a weather policy?
    Lessons proceed unless conditions are hazardous. We may cancel due to lightning, at our discretion.

    Other

    Can I tip my instructor?
    Tipping is optional but always appreciated.

    Where can I change?
    Restrooms are available on-site. Arrive early to allow time to change.
